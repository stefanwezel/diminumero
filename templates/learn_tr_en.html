Learn Turkish Numbers

This page covers Turkish number words. Turkish numbers are remarkably regular — there are no irregular teens or compound-word tricks. Learn the base words and you can build any number.

Digits: 0–9

  • 0 = sífír
  • 1 = bir
  • 2 = iki
  • 3 = üç
  • 4 = dört
  • 5 = beş
  • 6 = altı
  • 7 = yedi
  • 8 = sekiz
  • 9 = dokuz

10–99: tens

Simply say the tens word, then the ones word (with a space). No conjunctions needed:

  • 10 = on
  • 11 = on bir
  • 20 = yirmi
  • 30 = otuz
  • 40 = kırk
  • 50 = elli
  • 60 = altmış
  • 70 = yetmiş
  • 80 = seksen
  • 90 = doksan
  • 42 = kırk iki
  • 99 = doksan dokuz

Hundreds

  • 100 = yüz
  • 200 = iki yüz
  • 300 = üç yüz
  • 150 = yüz elli
  • 999 = dokuz yüz doksan dokuz

Note: 100 is just yüz (not “bir yüz”).

Thousands & large numbers

  • 1,000 = bin (not “bir bin”)
  • 2,000 = iki bin
  • 10,000 = on bin
  • 1,000,000 = bir milyon
  • 1,000,000,000 = bir milyar

Spelling & special characters

Turkish uses some characters not found in English:

  • ç — as in üç (3)
  • ş — as in beş (5)
  • ü — as in üç (3), yüz (100)
  • ö — as in dört (4)
  • ı (dotless i) — as in kırk (40), altı (6)
